Born in the Bay Area and raised in Stockton, I am a California girl through-and-through! I spent two and a half years at San Joaquin Delta College in Stockton, CA, where I graduated in the Fall of 2022 and earned my Associate's Degree in English. I have since transferred to BYU-Idaho, where I have been since January 2023, and am working on my Bachelor's in English with emphases on Creative Writing and Professional Writing.

In 2023, my short story "To Watch the Snow Fall" was published in the October issue of Austur Magazine, a Christian magazine based in Utah, and my story "Sea Glass" was published in Outlet, the student art and literature magazine at BYU-Idaho.

Recently, I have begun exploring other avenues in which I can develop and apply my written and visual communication skills, specifically through learning and using different forms of digital design, including using various Adobe programs.

You can reach me at samtoale@gmail.com or on LinkedIn!
